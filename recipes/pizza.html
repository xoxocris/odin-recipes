Recipe For Pizza

Ingredients

  • 16oz. Pizza Dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 12oz. mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 1/2 tsp. ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp. fresh oregano
  • Flour for rolling and shaping dough

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 500Â°F
  2. Roll out dough
  3. Add the toppings
  4. Cook the pizza for 6 minutes and rotate then cook for another 6 to 8 minutes.
  5. Slice and serve
